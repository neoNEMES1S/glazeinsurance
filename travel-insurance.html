Travel Insurance

Glaze Insurance, your reliable companion in the world of travel insurance. We understand that every journey comes with its share of uncertainties. Whether you're planning a leisure vacation, a business trip, or an adventure across the globe, our travel insurance plans offer you peace of mind, ensuring that you can explore the world with confidence.


Features of Travel Insurance

  • Comprehensive Travel Coverage:
    At Glaze Insurance, we offer a range of comprehensive travel insurance policies to suit different travel needs. Our plans cover everything from trip cancellations, medical emergencies, lost baggage, to unexpected travel interruptions, providing you with a safety net wherever you go.
  • Global Protection:
    Travel doesn't always go as planned, especially in unfamiliar territories. With our global network, we ensure you're protected no matter where you are in the world. Our international travel insurance covers you across borders, offering assistance 24/7, so you can focus on creating unforgettable memories.
  • Tailored Plans for Every Traveler:
    We understand that every traveler is unique. Whether you're a solo adventurer, a family explorer, a business executive, or a senior traveler, our range of policies can be customized to meet your specific requirements. We provide specialized plans catering to different age groups and travel purposes.
  • Emergency Assistance Services:
    In the face of an emergency, you're not alone. Our travel insurance comes with dedicated emergency assistance services. From medical referrals and evacuation to legal assistance, our team is just a phone call away, ensuring you receive the support you need during challenging situations.
  • Quick and Hassle-Free Claims:
    Filing a travel insurance claim shouldn't be a daunting task. Our streamlined claims process ensures that you can file and track claims online with ease. We value your time and aim to settle claims promptly, allowing you to focus on your journey to recovery.
  • Affordable Premiums:
    Travel insurance should be accessible to everyone. We offer competitive premiums without compromising on coverage. Our plans are designed to be affordable, making it easier for travelers of all budgets to enjoy the benefits of travel insurance.

Our Travel Insurance Plans are:

  • Single Trip Insurance:
    Ideal for one-time travelers.
    Provides coverage for a single trip, ensuring you're protected from unexpected events.
  • Annual Multi-Trip Insurance:
    Perfect for frequent travelers and business professionals.
    Offers year-round coverage for multiple trips, saving you time and money on individual policies.
  • Family Travel Insurance:
    Tailored for families traveling together.
    Protects your entire family under a single policy, offering peace of mind to parents and children alike.
  • Adventure and Sports Travel Insurance:
    For thrill-seekers and adventure enthusiasts.
    Provides specialized coverage for high-risk activities and extreme sports, ensuring your safety during adventurous pursuits.
