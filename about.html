Who are we ?

Experts in Providing Insurance Consulting Services

Glaze Insurance Broker Pvt. ltd. was incorporated in July’2021 and obtained its Certificate of Registration(COR: 834) from IRDAI on 11th July 2022 to act as a Direct Insurance Broker (Life and Non Life) . It is Headquartered in New Delhi and has presence in Bengaluru and Pune as well.

The Company is promoted by professionals from the field of Insurance and Finance with a clear commitment to service, small and medium enterprises, individuals and rural segment who have limited access to Insurance.

Our main objective is to get the best possible Insurance cover for our clients and give best in class services. We have professionals with long and proven experience to provide sound technical advice and best in class services.

Mission

Our mission is to provide the best Insurance cover to our clients at lowest cost possible bundled with best-in-class services and technical advise on optimal Insurance coverage. We deal in all classes of Insurance and are guided by the needs of our customers and design policies to mitigate their risk backed by professional services

Vision

Our Vision is to provide one stop solution to our customers for all their insurance needs and to protect and defend their interests. With changing times, we also evolve with our clients and are able to negotiate the best Insurance cover for our clients backed up by 24x7 professional services and accessibility of products on all platforms (F2F, Call centre, Web, Mobile platform). We offer concierge services to all our clients for policy delivery, cheque collection, claims submission and any other requirements.

Value

We value our customers and are neutral to the Insurers and their offerings and provide the best Insurance cover to our clients irrespective of our profitability. Customer services and ethical placements is our priority. All our employees and associates follow the ethical practice of not compromising on services amd cost optimality to our customers.
