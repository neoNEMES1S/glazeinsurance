Specialty Lines


Specialty insurance is a coverage plan designed for businesses that need very specific protections. These businesses may not normally be able to get coverage due to owning high-risk holdings or if they have properties or equipment that are not covered in a typical business insurance plan.


Examples of Specialty Insurance

  • Marine Insurance
  • Business Insurance
  • Auto Insurance (classic cars)
  • Flood Insurance
  • Umbrella/Excess Liability Insurance
