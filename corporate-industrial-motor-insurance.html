Corporate/Industrial Motor Insurance


Glaze Insurance, your dedicated partner in securing your corporate and industrial vehicles. We understand the vital role vehicles play in your business operations. With our comprehensive corporate motor insurance solutions, we offer you peace of mind, ensuring your fleet is protected against unforeseen events, allowing your business to operate smoothly and efficiently.



Features of Corporate/Industrial Motor Insurance:

  • Specialized Corporate Coverage:
    We specialize in providing tailored insurance solutions for corporate and industrial vehicles. Our comprehensive policies cover a wide range of vehicles, including trucks, vans, buses, and specialized industrial vehicles. We understand the unique risks associated with commercial operations and offer plans designed to protect your assets and investments.
  • Risk Management Expertise:
    Our team of insurance professionals has extensive experience in corporate risk management. We assess your specific needs, identify potential risks, and tailor insurance solutions that provide optimal coverage. With our expertise, you can focus on your core business activities, knowing that your vehicles are well-protected.
  • Quick Claim Processing:
    Minimizing downtime is crucial for businesses. We ensure a streamlined claims process, allowing you to report and process claims swiftly. Our dedicated claims team works efficiently to resolve claims, enabling your vehicles to return to operation promptly.
  • Fleet Management Support:
    Beyond insurance, we offer fleet management support services. Our experts provide guidance on best practices, vehicle maintenance, driver safety, and compliance with regulations. By partnering with us, you receive comprehensive support for your corporate fleet.
  • Customized Policies:
    We understand that every business is unique. Our policies are flexible and can be customized to match your business requirements. Whether you need coverage for a small fleet of delivery vans or a large industrial fleet, we create policies tailored to your fleet's size, usage, and specific needs.
  • Competitive Pricing:
    We offer competitive premiums without compromising on coverage. Our corporate motor insurance plans are designed to be cost-effective, ensuring you receive excellent value for your investment. We work with you to find a balance between comprehensive coverage and budget considerations.

Our Corporate Motor Insurance Covers:

  • Comprehensive Vehicle Coverage:
    Protects your corporate vehicles against damages from accidents, theft, natural disasters, and vandalism.
    Includes repairs, replacements, and other associated costs.
  • Third-Party Liability:
    Covers damages and injuries caused to third parties in an accident involving your corporate vehicles.
    Provides coverage for legal defense costs and settlement fees.
  • Business Interruption Coverage:
    Offers financial protection in case your business operations are interrupted due to a covered incident.
    Covers revenue loss, additional expenses, and ongoing operating costs.
  • Specialized Vehicle Coverage:
    Provides coverage for specialized industrial vehicles, including cranes, bulldozers, and forklifts.
    Ensures that all equipment crucial for your operations is protected.
